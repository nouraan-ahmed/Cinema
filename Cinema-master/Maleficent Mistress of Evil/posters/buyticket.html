How To Buy Your Cinema Tickets

Get your cinema tickets from our ‘Eventbrite’ page here:

We only sell tickets in advance for our next available film. Check back for future events! Tickets for special events may not be available online. Please be aware popular events may sell out in advance of the event so if you know you want to come, grab a ticket!

Don’t want to pay the booking fee? You can also buy tickets in person from New Earswick Folk Hall, York.

New Earswick Folk Hall
Hawthorn Terrace
New Earswick
York,
YO32 4AQ
01904 769621
folkhall@jrf.org.uk

Reception open for ticket sales Monday – Friday 8.30am – 4.30pm

We also have a lovely cafe open from Monday  to Saturday.

Sorry, the Folk Hall is closed on Sundays.
